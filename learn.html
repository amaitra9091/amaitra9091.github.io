Who are We as People?

The Basillica of Rome

The basilican architectural style originated in ancient Rome and was originally used for public buildings where courts were held, as well as serving other official and public functions. The basilica was centrally located in every Roman town, usually adjacent to the main forum.

Palatine Hill

is the centremost of the Seven Hills of Rome and is one of the most ancient parts of the city. It stands 40 metres above the Roman Forum, looking down upon it on one side, and upon the Circus Maximus on the other. We have a top class view from our office

Consuls

A consul was the highest elected political office of the Roman Republic, and the consulship was considered the highest level an ascending sequence of public offices to which politicians aspired.

The Roman Ampitheater

Roman Ampitheaters are large, circular or oval open-air venues with raised seating. They are used for events such as gladiator combats, animal slayings and executions. About 230 Roman amphitheatres have been found across the area of the Roman Empire.

Science during Our Times

We make great use of aqueducts, dams, bridges, and amphitheatres. We are also responsible for many innovations to roads, sanitation, and construction in general.
